PC-88 club!

8-bit fun from Japan

The PC-88 was the premier home computer in Japan through the 1980's. It's superior sound and modern, colorful graphics propelled it far ahead of the competition for the next decade.

We have collected some of the greatest games and software created for NEC's legendary machine as well as emulators for Windows, Linux/BSD, android and iPhone.
